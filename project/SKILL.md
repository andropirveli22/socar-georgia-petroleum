## Quick reference

- **Brand colors:** SOCAR Red `#E2231A` (energy), SOCAR Green `#00A551` (sustainability), SOCAR Blue `#0099D8` (gas/tech), Ink `#0A0A0A`. See `colors_and_type.css`.
- **Type:** Inter Tight (display), Inter (body), Noto Sans Georgian for ქართული. Substitutes — replace with canonical brand fonts when supplied.
- **Voice:** Corporate, operational, infrastructure-grade. Numbers and scale do the talking. No emoji, no slang, no exclamation marks.
- **Logos:** `assets/logos/socar-flame.png` (official three-flame mark, 512×512 transparent). Pair with the SOCAR wordmark in Inter Tight 800, letter-spacing ~1.4px. Request vector for print.or production.
- **Icons:** Lucide 2px stroke (CDN substitute).
- **UI kits:** `ui_kits/website/` (corporate site), `ui_kits/station/` (totem, pump screen, wayfinding).
- **Cards/previews:** `preview/*.html` show all foundations at a glance.
